Acrylic vs Oils

Acrylics Oils
For acrylics, acrylic polymer emulsion a binder. For oil paint,linseed oil or other drying oil is a binder.
Fast drying time. Slow drying time but can be useful for certain tchniques.
Water is used as a solvent for acrylic paint. Solvents such as mineral spirits or turpentine are used to thin paint and clean up.
Acrylics can be safely applied staright to the canvas. Before painting with oil on canvas,canvas needs to be properly primed to prvent from rooting the canvas.
Acrylics are very versatile and are useful in mixed media. Oils are not that versatile.
Acrylic paint can be cleaned out of a brush with any soap. oil paint needs a specific type to be sure to get all the oil out of the brushes.
